About Newcastle First

A free, community-driven business networking movement built on one simple idea — the best connections shouldn't come with a price tag.

Who We Are

A community built on real connection

Newcastle First is a relaxed, informal gathering for local business leaders who want to put Newcastle first, share ideas, and explore new opportunities for collaboration.

Whether you're looking to grow your network, spark partnerships, or simply stay in the loop with fellow innovators — this is the place to be. All business sectors welcome. Just bring your curiosity and community spirit.

We are one of very few completely free networking events across the entire North East region. By removing the barriers to entry, we open the door to young entrepreneurs just getting started and experienced business leaders alike.

100% Free to Attend — no membership fees, no hidden costs, no long-term commitments.

Our Values

What drives us

Community First

We put the people of Newcastle and the North East at the heart of everything we do. Real relationships over transactions.

No Barriers

Everyone deserves a seat at the table. We remove financial and social barriers so anyone can connect and grow.

Real Social Value

We're not just about business cards. We generate genuine social value — inspiring stories, practical support, and lasting impact.

Growth for All

From first-time founders to seasoned CEOs — we bridge the gap between ambition and experience so everyone grows together.

Why Newcastle?

The beating heart of
the North East

Newcastle is home to world-class universities, a thriving tech and creative sector, and a business community full of ambition. With major investment in city infrastructure and the ongoing regeneration of the Quayside, Newcastle has never been better positioned for business.

World-Class Universities

Newcastle and Northumbria Universities produce thousands of graduates and research breakthroughs annually.

Thriving Tech & Creative Sector

A growing hub for digital, creative, and tech businesses — attracting investment from across the UK and beyond.

Major City Investment

Quayside regeneration, transport upgrades, and major commercial developments are transforming Newcastle's business landscape.

Our Mission

Building a community that generates real opportunity

We're building a business community that is first and foremost supportive — and in turn becomes progressive and generative.

Generating sales, generating income, and generating real opportunities for businesses across the region. We want to connect emerging young entrepreneurs with established, experienced business leaders — bridging the gap and creating a network where everyone grows together.

See Our Projects
The Team Behind Newcastle First

Proudly independent

Newcastle First is run by local people who care deeply about this city and its business community. We're not a faceless corporation — we're your neighbours, your collaborators, your fellow North Easterners.

We believe Darlington First, Newcastle First and the wider First Connections network can help the North East punch above its weight on the national stage — starting with the grassroots, one coffee morning at a time.

Just a heads-up: While our events are free, we ask everyone to pay for their own coffee. It's our way of supporting the brilliant local venues who host us.
