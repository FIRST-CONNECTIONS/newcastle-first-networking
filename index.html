Welcome

Welcome to Newcastle First

This is a relaxed, informal gathering for local business leaders who want to put Newcastle first, share ideas, and explore new opportunities for collaboration. Whether you're looking to grow your network, spark partnerships, or simply stay in the loop with fellow innovators, this event is the place to be.

All business sectors welcome — just bring your curiosity and community spirit!

✓  100% Free to Attend
No Barriers. No Fees.

Just Community.

We believe the best business connections shouldn't come with a price tag. Newcastle First events are completely free to attend — one of very few free networking events across the entire North East region.

By removing the barriers to entry, we're opening the door for young entrepreneurs just getting started and experienced business leaders alike. Everyone deserves a seat at the table.

Our networking events will vary — some traditional networking, others more creative: think watching local football or rugby together, a game of padel or tennis, bowling, and plenty more. The one thing we're committed to is a free, no-hassle monthly coffee morning — with venues rotating each month to support as many local Newcastle businesses as possible.

☕ Just a heads-up: While our events are free, we do ask that everyone pays for their own coffee. It's our little way of supporting the brilliant local venues who host us.

Why Newcastle?

Why Newcastle?

Newcastle is the beating heart of the North East — home to world-class universities, a thriving tech and creative sector, and a business community full of ambition. With major investment in city infrastructure and the ongoing regeneration of the Quayside and city centre, Newcastle has never been better positioned for business.

The opportunity is enormous. And we believe Newcastle is ready to become the powerhouse of business networking and connections across the entire North East region.

Our Mission

Building a community that generates real opportunity

About Newcastle First

A free, community-driven business networking movement built on one simple idea — the best connections shouldn't come with a price tag.

Who We Are

A community built on real connection

Newcastle First is a relaxed, informal gathering for local business leaders who want to put Newcastle first, share ideas, and explore new opportunities for collaboration.

Whether you're looking to grow your network, spark partnerships, or simply stay in the loop with fellow innovators — this is the place to be. All business sectors welcome. Just bring your curiosity and community spirit.

We are one of very few completely free networking events across the entire North East region. By removing the barriers to entry, we open the door to young entrepreneurs just getting started and experienced business leaders alike.

✓  100% Free to Attend — no membership fees, no hidden costs, no long-term commitments.

Our Values

What drives us

🤝 Community First

We put the people of Newcastle and the North East at the heart of everything we do. Real relationships over transactions.

Everyone deserves a seat at the table. We remove financial and social barriers so anyone can connect and grow.

We're not just about business cards. We generate genuine social value — inspiring stories, practical support, and lasting impact.

🌱 Growth for All

From first-time founders to seasoned CEOs — we bridge the gap between ambition and experience so everyone grows together.
